FAQs

Can I access services remotely?

Yes, InnerWhispers provides virtual counseling and therapy sessions via video calls, allowing you to access support from the comfort of your home.

Is my information confidential?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy. All information shared during sessions is kept confidential and secure, adhering to HIPAA guidelines and best practices.

How can I make payments for services?

Payments can be made securely through our website using various payment methods, including credit/debit cards and online payment platforms.

What should I expect during my first session?

In your first session, the therapist will ask questions to understand your concerns and goals, explain the therapy process, and discuss how they can support you.
