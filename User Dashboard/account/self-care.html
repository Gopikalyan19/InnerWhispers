    Self-Care Resources

    Yoga for Stress Relief and Anxiety
    Short, beginner-friendly yoga sequences focused on mental and physical well-being. Yoga can help users relax, improve flexibility, and focus on breathing, which reduces anxiety and stress.
    10-20 minutes
    Mindfulness Meditation for Emotional Balance
    Guided meditation videos that help users practice mindfulness and deep relaxation. These meditations can assist in lowering stress, improving focus, and enhancing emotional well-being.
    5-15 minutes
    PMR for Headache Relief and Relaxation
    Guided exercises that teach users how to tense and relax specific muscle groups. This technique helps reduce physical tension and mental stress.
    10-15 minutes
