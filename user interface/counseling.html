Counseling

Counselling is a unique opportunity to talk to a trained professional about any difficult feelings you might be having at some point we can all feel overwhelmed by feelings of sadness anger and anxiety but it can be hard talking about it to the people closest to us.

This might be because we don't want to upset them or maybe we're worried how they'll react.

Counselling provides you with the time and space to explore these feelings you can tell your counselor anything without worrying about their feelings no matter what you tell them they'll never judge you your counselor will never tell you what to do they'll simply help you to work through your feelings. so they become easier to manage to help you with this they may suggest activities or strategies which will help you with these feelings.

However the counselling sessions are yours to use as you need them if there's things you'd like to work on in the sessions just let your counsellor know maybe you're struggling with your feelings because you're finding it hard to get on with the people in your life such as your parents or teachers you can talk about this with your counsellor and they will always keep it confidential.
